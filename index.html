Gabe Newell, often called as "Gaben" or "God", is a co-founder and the president of Valve Corporation, a video game development and digital distribution company. The company is known worldwide for AAA titles such as Half-Life and Half-Life 2, and also for not developing Half-Life 3.

Here are some mentionable events in Gabe's life:

  • 1962 - The legend itself was born
  • 1980 - Joined Harvard University
  • 1983 - Dropped out of Harvard to work at Microsoft
  • 1996 - Left Microsoft and founded Valve Corporation
  • 1998 - Released Half-Life
  • 2004 - Released Half-Life 2, along with the Source Engine
  • 2013 - Officially Released Dota 2, built upon the Source Engine
  • 2017 - Still haven't released Half-Life 3
